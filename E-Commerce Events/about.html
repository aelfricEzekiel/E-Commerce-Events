About LINGAW Music Fest

LINGAW word derived from people in Visayas whereas it means fun. This MusicFest centers to make people have fun through a lot of music categories such as: OPM, Indie, Rock, Jazz and etc. This event serves not just simple fun but a good event for people to interact with other people, eat through our food stalls, camp in the open area, and many more. For more informations and update about the MusicFest, we empower each participant to log in on our website. Come on, Let's have some fun!
